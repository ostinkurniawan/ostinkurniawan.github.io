Hi, I'm Ostin.

Hi, I'm Ostin and I'm a designer, researcher and student studying at the University of Washington-Seattle, pursuing a degree in Human-Centered Design & Engineering.

My primary interests are in social media design and research, specifically the way information flows across platforms and the greater web. I currently contribute to the emCOMP (Emerging Capacities of Mass Participation) lab with Dr. Kate Starbird, and am working on housing visualizations at CoSSaR (Center for Collaborative Systems for Security, Safety, and Regional Resilience) with Dr. Scott Miles. 

I also serve as a layout designer at Voyage, a grassroots travel magazine, and as graphic design / marketing at the Expatriate Student Association (Dub Ex).Having lived across the United States and a decade abroad, I am passionate about multiculturalism, diversity, and perspective. I am always up for learning new about new cultures, new languages, and new people.
